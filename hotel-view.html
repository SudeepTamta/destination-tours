About Beleza by the beach

CHECK IN 2 PM . CHECK OUT 11 AM

Set right by the calming golden sands of Colva, Beleza By The Beach offers a dreamy tropical stay, enriched with unique services and luxury rooms housed in gorgeous villas.

The resort features two outdoor pools with a dedicated small pool for kids.

Enjoy the comfort of private balconies and outdoor bathtubs that offer views of lush green fields.

Spend a lovely evening at the beachfront restaurant with scrumptious seafood and live music performances.

The in-house spa offers rejuvenating Swedish and Balinese massages with steam sessions.
