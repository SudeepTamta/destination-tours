Cancellation Information

For cancellation, no penalty till 2 hours before departure, 50% up to Rs 1000 within 2 hours of departure & 100% penalty beyond departure time.

Passenger Travel Guidelines

Complete lockdown has been imposed in Uttarakhand till 25th May, 2021. E-Pass is required for travel to/from any city within Uttarakhand and can be downloaded from: http://smartcitydehradun.uk.gov.in/ Those travelling to/from Uttarakhand must carry a negative RT-PCR test report, conducted within 72 hours before travel. The e-Pass and negative RT-PCR test report (as mentioned above) is required even though the traveller might have a valid flight or train ticket. The e-Pass has to be shared with the driver before the trip starts. Without it, the driver will not be able to reach the pickup location. 2 passengers + 1 driver will be allowed to travel in sedan vehicles and 3 passengers + 1 driver in SUVs.

Select Indian States & UTs have enforced specific travel guidelines. See Guidelines
